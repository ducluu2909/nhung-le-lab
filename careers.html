Application Process

A transparent and supportive approach to joining our research team

01

Application Review

Submit your CV and publications (if you have any). Every application is carefully reviewed by our research team members.

02

Initial Interview

We begin with an interview to understand your background, research interests, and how you align with our lab's research directions.

03

Research Discussion

Present your previous research or discuss potential projects. This helps us understand your technical expertise and research approach.

04

Lab Visit & Team Meeting

Meet with lab members, tour our facilities, and get a feel for our research environment and collaborative culture.

05

Decision & Onboarding

If there's mutual fit, we'll make an offer and help you transition smoothly into our research team.

Note: We provide feedback within 1-2 weeks. We welcome Bachelor's and Master's students with or without research experience.
