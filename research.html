Transforming Drug Discovery

At Nhung Le's Molecular Biology Lab, we combine cutting-edge artificial intelligence with deep expertise in molecular biology to accelerate the discovery of life-saving therapeutics. Our interdisciplinary approach integrates computational modeling, structural biology, and clinical translation to address the world's most challenging diseases.

Molecular Biology

Understanding protein structures, interactions, and disease mechanisms at the molecular level

Bioinformatics

Integrating AI, machine learning, and computational methods to analyze biological data, predict molecular properties, and accelerate drug discovery for complex diseases

Biomaterials

Engineering novel biomaterials and biochips for advanced diagnostic platforms, drug delivery systems, and tissue engineering applications

Magnetic Beads for Enrichment of Botulinum Toxins

Principal Investigator: M.Sc. Hieu Luong

In this study, for the first time, nanobody conjugated beads were developed to enrich botulinum serotype A. We evaluated two types of nanobody A8-functionalized magnetic beads, both of which effectively captured and enriched BoNT/A from dilute solutions and complex protein mixtures, achieving a concentration increase of approximately 12- to 30-fold.

Key Objectives

  • Synthesis of Antibody and Antigen
  • Evaluating binding activity of antibody-botulinum complex
  • Detect botulinum using magnetic beads
  • Industrial application
