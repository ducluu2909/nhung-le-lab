Meet the people leading our lab

We're here to find solutions to the world's most devastating diseases

Advisors and Post-Bachelor

Nhung Le

Principal Investigator

Nhung Le leads the Molecular Biology Lab with expertise in bioinformatics, material biology, and molecular biology research.

Her research focuses on integrating computational methods with experimental biology to advance drug discovery and bio-materials development.

She established the lab to foster interdisciplinary collaboration across three key departments: Bioinformatics, Material Biology, and Molecular Biology.

"There is no rejection in education."
